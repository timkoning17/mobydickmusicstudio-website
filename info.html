About the Studio

Moby Dick Music Studio is a recording studio in Schieweg, Delft. The room is built around one idea: source is king. Great instruments, a great space, real preamps and hardware compressors in the signal path.

From a Yamaha C5 grand piano and Hammond A-100 to a rack full of classic synths, vintage guitars, and a well-tuned drum room. The gear here actually gets used.

What We Offer

Whether you're a band cutting a live record, a producer layering overdubs, or a solo artist demoing new material, Moby Dick has the space and the tools to make it happen.

We work with artists across all genres. The setup is hybrid: real hardware where it matters, in the room and at the console, combined with a full digital workflow so you leave with files that are ready to go.

The Rooms

The studio has a main live room and several additional recording spaces, giving you flexibility for different configurations: full band, isolation, or anything in between. All rooms feed into 32 analog inputs at the console.

Rates & Booking

Rates are flexible and discussed per project. Get in touch via the booking form or by phone and we'll work out what fits.

Location

We're at Schieweg 15D, 2627AN Delft. Easily reachable by car, with parking on-site. Close to the A13 motorway.
