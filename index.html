The Studio

Source is king. Everything starts here.

Moby Dick Music Studio is a recording studio in Schieweg, Delft. We believe the best recordings start with great instruments in a great room, captured through real preamps and hardware that actually does something to the sound.

From grand piano and Hammond to vintage synths and a full live room. Everything you need to make something that sounds like something is right here.
